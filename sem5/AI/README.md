# 🎯 Course Overview

Artificial Intelligence (AI) focuses on the development of intelligent systems capable of simulating human decision-making, reasoning, learning, and problem-solving. The course introduces core AI concepts, machine learning fundamentals, knowledge representation, intelligent agents, and real-world AI applications.

Throughout the semester, our team developed an AI-powered healthcare prototype designed to assist users in obtaining preliminary health assessments based on symptoms and medical information.

---

# 🚀 Main Project: AI Diagnosis Application Prototype

## Project Overview

The main project involved designing and developing a prototype AI Diagnosis Application capable of providing preliminary health assessments based on symptoms entered by users.

The application aims to support healthcare awareness by helping users identify potential health conditions and encouraging them to seek professional medical consultation when necessary.

---

# 📂 Project Features

## 🩺 Symptom Analysis

Users can input symptoms experienced and receive preliminary diagnostic suggestions generated by the system.

### Features

- Symptom Input Form
- Symptom Matching
- Disease Recommendation
- Health Information Display
- User-Friendly Interface

## 🤖 AI-Based Decision Support

The prototype incorporates AI concepts to analyze user inputs and generate possible diagnostic outcomes.

The system provides users with additional information regarding identified health conditions.

# 💡 Reflection

Artificial Intelligence was one of the most interesting courses because it introduced how machines can simulate human reasoning and decision-making processes. Through the AI Diagnosis Application project, I learned that AI is not only about advanced machine learning models but also about designing systems capable of making logical decisions based on available knowledge.

Developing the prototype strengthened my understanding of problem analysis, knowledge representation, and decision-support systems. I learned how AI can be applied to solve real-world challenges in healthcare by assisting users in identifying potential health conditions and increasing awareness of preventive care.

The project also improved my teamwork, communication, and system design skills. Working on the prototype required translating user requirements into a practical solution while ensuring the system remained understandable and user-friendly.

Most importantly, this course broadened my perspective on the impact of AI across industries and reinforced the importance of developing responsible and ethical AI solutions that assist rather than replace human expertise.
