# 🎯 Course Overview

Business Intelligence (BI) focuses on transforming raw data into meaningful insights that support organizational decision-making. The course covers business problem identification, data preparation, data transformation, analytical processing, and dashboard development using modern BI tools.

Throughout the semester, a comprehensive project was conducted using a **Flight Delay Dataset**, progressing through three major phases: business understanding, data preparation, and dashboard visualization.

---

# 📂 Project: Flight Delay Analytics Dashboard

## ✈️ Phase 1: Business Understanding & Problem Analysis

### Project Description

The first phase focused on understanding the business problem and analyzing the Flight Delay Dataset.

The project aimed to investigate:

- Factors contributing to flight delays.
- Airlines with the highest delay rates.
- Airport performance and operational efficiency.
- Delay trends and patterns.
- Potential business impacts of flight delays.

### Key Learning Areas

- Business Problem Identification
- Data Understanding
- Requirement Analysis
- KPI Identification
- Analytical Thinking

### Reflection

This phase taught me that successful analytics projects begin with a clear understanding of the business problem rather than immediately focusing on data. I learned how to translate business questions into measurable analytical objectives and define meaningful KPIs for decision-making.

---

## 🧹 Phase 2: Data Cleaning and Transformation using Alteryx

### Project Description

The second phase involved preparing the Flight Delay Dataset using Alteryx.

Activities included:

- Data Cleaning
- Missing Value Handling
- Data Transformation
- Data Validation
- Data Integration
- Data Preparation for Reporting

### Key Learning Areas

- Alteryx Workflow Development
- Data Cleansing
- Data Transformation
- Data Quality Management
- ETL Processes

### Reflection

This phase highlighted the importance of data quality in business intelligence projects. I learned that even the most advanced dashboards can produce misleading insights if the underlying data is inaccurate or incomplete. Alteryx significantly improved my understanding of automated data preparation workflows.

---

## 📊 Phase 3: Dashboard Development using Power BI

### Project Description

The final phase focused on creating interactive dashboards and visual reports using Power BI.

The dashboard was designed to provide insights into:

- Flight Delay Trends
- Airline Performance
- Airport Performance
- Delay Categories
- Operational KPIs
- Data-Driven Decision Support

### Key Learning Areas

- Power BI Development
- Data Visualization
- Dashboard Design
- KPI Monitoring
- Interactive Reporting
- Storytelling with Data

### Reflection

This phase demonstrated how visualization transforms processed data into actionable business insights. I learned how effective dashboard design helps stakeholders quickly identify trends, monitor performance, and make informed decisions.

---

# 💡 Overall Reflection

Business Intelligence has been one of the most practical courses in my degree because it combines business understanding, data preparation, and visualization into a complete analytical workflow.

The Flight Delay Analytics project allowed me to experience the full business intelligence lifecycle, beginning with problem identification, followed by data preparation in Alteryx, and ending with dashboard development in Power BI.

One of the most important lessons I learned is that business intelligence is not only about creating dashboards but also about understanding business objectives and ensuring that data accurately supports decision-making. The course strengthened both my technical and analytical skills while improving my ability to communicate insights effectively through visualization.

As a future Data Engineer, this course helped me understand how data pipelines and data preparation processes directly contribute to business intelligence solutions used by decision-makers.
