# 🎯 Course Overview

Technology Information System (TIS) introduces students to the fundamental concepts of information systems and their role in organizations. The course explores computer hardware, software, networking, databases, digital transformation, emerging technologies, and the impact of information systems on businesses and society.

Throughout the semester, various assignments, practical activities, and industrial talks were conducted to provide both theoretical knowledge and real-world exposure to technology applications.
